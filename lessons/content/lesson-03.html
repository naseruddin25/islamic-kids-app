Think of Islam Like a House

Allah teaches us that when belief becomes corrupted, even good-looking deeds are rejected and worship loses its value.

Why Belief Comes First

If someone prays but doesn't believe in Allah, the prayer doesn't count. If belief is corrupted, worship is not accepted by Allah ‚Äî even if it looks good outwardly.

This is why scholars say: Belief must be correct first. Then actions follow.

‚≠ê Key Takeaway

Islamic belief (Aqeedah) is the foundation of Islam. Without a correct foundation, nothing stands.

The six pillars of faith are:

  • The Oneness of Allah
  • The Angels of Allah
  • The Books of Allah
  • The Messengers of Allah
  • The Day of Judgment
  • The Divine Decree (Qadr)

These come from the Qur'an and Sunnah. Believe them with certainty. Everything else in Islam builds on them.

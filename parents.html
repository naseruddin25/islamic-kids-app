Supporting Independent Learning

A conversation starter, not a grading tool. Encourage discussion and critical thinking.

How to Use This Site

1. Let Them Choose

Teens can browse lessons independently. Recommend starting with Foundations or topics that interest them.

2. Review Together (Optional)

If they want to discuss what they learned, review the lesson together. Ask open-ended questions.

3. Use Quizzes as Check-Ins

Quizzes are self-check tools, not tests. Results can spark conversations about what they understood.
